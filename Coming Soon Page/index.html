About Annie's Pharm

Annie's Pharm is a locally owned and operated professional medicinal cannabis farm in Oklahoma. We strive for the the highest quality product on our indoor and outdoor operations while reducing environmental impacts.
We also provide consulting services to help with old or new grow operations in the state of Oklahoma.
